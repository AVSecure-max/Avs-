privacy policy

AVS Privacy Policy Effective Date: July 2, 2026 At AVS WE, protecting your privacy is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have when using the AVS browser extension, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). Our mission is to help people verify online information while respecting their privacy.

Information We Collect

Information You Choose to Provide

    Depending on how you use AVS, you may provide:
  • Your email address if you create an account or contact support.
  • Feedback you voluntarily submit.
  • Information you provide when reporting incorrect verification results.
    • Information Processed for Verification
      When you ask AVS to verify content, the extension may securely process:
  • The webpage URL.
  • Selected text or webpage content necessary for verification.
  • Metadata required to perform verification requests.
  • AVS only processes the information necessary to generate verification results.
    • Technical Information
      To operate and improve the Services, we may collect:
  • Browser type and version.
  • Operating system.
  • Extension version.
  • Anonymous device identifiers.
  • Error logs and crash reports.
  • Performance diagnostics.
    • We strive to minimize the collection of technical information wherever possible.

    Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

    is designed to avoid collecting unnecessary personal information.

      Unless required for the specific verification request, we do not intentionally collect:
  • Passwords.
  • Credit card or payment information.
  • Banking information.
  • Private messages.
  • Contacts.
  • Photos.
  • Microphone recordings.
  • Camera recordings.
  • Complete browsing history.
    • If such information is unintentionally included in content submitted for verification, it is processed only as needed to provide the requested verification service.

    How We Use Information

      We use collected information to:
  • Verify online content.
  • Generate AI-powered verification results.
  • Improve verification accuracy.
  • Detect bugs and technical issues.
  • Maintain security.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
    • We do not build advertising profiles based on your browsing activity.

    AI Processing

    Verification requests may be processed using artificial intelligence technologies operated by AVS or trusted third-party service providers. Content submitted for verification is processed solely for the purpose of generating verification results and improving the reliability of the Services where permitted.

    Data Sharing

      We may share information only in limited circumstances:
  • With trusted service providers who help operate AVS.
  • With cloud infrastructure providers.
  • With AI service providers that process verification requests on our behalf.
  • When required by law.
  • To protect the security, rights, or safety of users or AVS.
    • We do not sell personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

    Data Retention

    We retain information only for as long as necessary to: Provide the Services. Improve system reliability. Meet legal obligations. Resolve disputes. Enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it where reasonably practicable.

    Security

    We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, including: Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Secure cloud infrastructure. Access controls. Authentication safeguards. Monitoring for unauthorized access. Regular security improvements. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to protect user information.

    Your Choices

    Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to: Access your information. Correct inaccurate information. Request deletion of your information. Object to certain processing activities. Withdraw consent where applicable. You may also disable or uninstall the AVS browser extension at any time.

    Browser Extension Permissions

    The AVS browser extension requests only the permissions reasonably necessary to perform verification services. These permissions may include: Reading webpage content when you request verification. Accessing the active browser tab. Communicating securely with AVS servers. We do not use extension permissions for unrelated purposes.

    Children's Privacy

    AVS is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate authorization where required by law. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

    International Users

    Your information may be processed in countries where AVS or its service providers operate. We take appropriate measures designed to protect personal information during international transfers where required.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the Effective Date and provide notice through the Services where appropriate. Your continued use of AVS after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
