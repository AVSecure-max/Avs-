About AVS

AVS is an AI-powered verification platform built by teens dedicated to building trust in the world by helping people instantly verify information, sources, and online content. In an age of misinformation, we're committed to making verification accessible to everyone.

Problem We Are Solving

trust in what you hear read listen and watch. Every day, businesses and individuals make important decisions based on information they find online. But the internet is increasingly filled with AI-generated content, misinformation, manipulated media, outdated information, and fraudulent documents. Verifying whether something is trustworthy often requires opening multiple tabs, searching for sources, and spending valuable time fact-checking. For procurement teams, finance professionals, compliance departments, and organizations handling high-value decisions, a single piece of false information can lead to financial loss, fraud, compliance risks, reputational damage, and poor business decisions. Current verification tools are often fragmented, slow, or require users to leave their workflow. Many focus on only one type of content—such as text, images, or websites—rather than providing comprehensive, real-time verification where people already work. As AI-generated content becomes more advanced and difficult to distinguish from authentic information, trust is becoming one of the biggest challenges facing businesses today. AVS exists to help solve this problem by making verification fast, accessible, and integrated directly into the user's workflow—so decisions can be made with greater confidence.

Our solution

Instead of spending valuable time manually checking multiple sources, users can verify content directly within their workflow. Through our browser extension and future cross-platform applications, AVS analyzes websites, documents, text, images, videos, and other digital content to identify potential misinformation, inconsistencies, and signs of manipulation. Users receive clear verification results, supporting evidence, confidence indicators, and a downloadable verification log to improve transparency and decision-making. Built with procurement, finance, compliance, risk, and enterprise teams in mind, AVS helps organizations reduce fraud risk, improve due diligence, and make faster, more informed decisions. Our long-term vision is to become the trusted verification layer for the internet—helping people and organizations verify information instantly, confidently, and at scale.
